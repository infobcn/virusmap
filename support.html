‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which regions does VirusMap cover?
VirusMap provides health data exclusively for Catalonia, Spain. Data comes from the Public Health Agency of Catalonia and other official sources.
Where does the data come from?
All data is obtained from official public health sources like the Public Health Agency of Catalonia. We do not collect personal health data from users.
How do AI predictions work?
Our predictions use machine learning to analyze historical trends and project potential future outbreaks. Each prediction includes a confidence level to help you interpret the results.
How often is data updated?
Data syncs with official sources. Update frequency depends on when health authorities publish new data, typically several times per week.
Does the app work offline?
VirusMap stores data locally on your device, so you can view previously downloaded data without connection. However, you need internet to fetch the latest updates.
Is the app free?
Yes, VirusMap is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
How is my privacy protected?
All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal health information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
What if I find a bug?
If you find a bug or technical issue, email us at app@xenapp.ro with a description of the problem and screenshots if possible.
